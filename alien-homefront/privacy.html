Alien Homefront Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 11, 2026

Overview

Alien Homefront is an iPhone arcade defense game developed by Leegan Swafford. This policy explains what information the app handles and how it is used.

Game Data

Alien Homefront stores gameplay progress on your device, including settings, high scores, upgrades, difficulty, saved runs, and similar game state. This data is used to operate the game and remember your progress.

Advertising

The app may show ads through Google AdMob. AdMob may process device identifiers, approximate location, advertising interaction data, diagnostics, and similar information according to Google's policies. If App Tracking Transparency permission is requested, you can allow or deny tracking in the system prompt or later in iPhone Settings.

No Account Required

Alien Homefront does not require you to create an account and does not ask for your name, email address, phone number, or password.

Children

Alien Homefront is a general arcade game and is not intended to collect personal information from children.

Changes

This policy may be updated from time to time. If changes are made, the date at the top of this page will be updated.

Contact

For privacy or support questions, email leegan.swafford@proton.me.
